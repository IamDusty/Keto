What is the Keto Diet?

The ketogenic (keto) diet is a low-carb, high-fat diet that can help you burn fat more effectively. It has many benefits for weight loss, health, and performance.

The keto diet drastically reduces carbohydrate intake and replaces it with fat. This reduction in carbs puts your body into a metabolic state called ketosis, where it becomes incredibly efficient at burning fat for energy.

When following a keto diet, your body switches its fuel supply to run mostly on fat, burning fat 24-7. When insulin levels drop, fat burning can increase dramatically, making it easier to access your fat stores to burn them off.

Daily Carb Limits

For most people, a keto diet means restricting carbohydrate intake to:

  • 20-50g of net carbs per day
  • Net carbs = Total carbs - Fiber

Common Keto Foods

Keto-Friendly Foods

  • Eggs
  • Meat (beef, pork, lamb)
  • Poultry (chicken, turkey)
  • Fatty fish (salmon, mackerel)
  • Full-fat dairy (cheese, butter, cream)
  • Low-carb vegetables (leafy greens, broccoli)
  • Nuts and seeds (almonds, walnuts, flaxseeds)
  • Avocados
  • Olive oil and coconut oil

Non-Keto Foods

  • Grains (wheat, rice, corn, oats)
  • Sugar and sugary foods
  • Fruits (except small portions of berries)
  • Starchy vegetables (potatoes, sweet potatoes)
  • Legumes (beans, lentils, peas)
  • Processed foods with added sugars
  • Most condiments and sauces
  • Alcohol (beer, sweet wines)

Popular Keto Substitutions

Missing your favorite non-keto foods? Try these keto-friendly alternatives!

Rice Alternatives

  • Cauliflower Rice - 3g carbs per cup
  • Broccoli Rice - 4g carbs per cup
  • Shirataki Rice - 1g carbs per cup

Tip: Saut√© cauliflower rice with butter and herbs for extra flavor.

Pasta Alternatives

  • Zucchini Noodles - 3g carbs per cup
  • Shirataki Noodles - 0.5g carbs per cup
  • Spaghetti Squash - 7g carbs per cup

Tip: Don't overcook veggie noodles or they'll become mushy.

Bread Alternatives

  • Almond Flour Bread - 3g carbs per slice
  • Cloud Bread - 0.5g carbs per piece
  • Flaxseed Bread - 1.5g carbs per slice

Tip: Add psyllium husk to keto bread for better texture.

Potato Alternatives

  • Mashed Cauliflower - 4g carbs per cup
  • Turnips - 6g carbs per cup
  • Radishes - 2g carbs per cup

Tip: Add cream cheese to mashed cauliflower for creaminess.

Sugar Alternatives

  • Erythritol - 0g net carbs per tbsp
  • Stevia - 0g carbs per tsp
  • Monk Fruit - 0g carbs per tsp

Tip: Blend sweeteners for better taste and fewer side effects.

Milk Alternatives

  • Almond Milk - 1g carbs per cup
  • Coconut Milk - 2g carbs per cup
  • Heavy Cream - 0.5g carbs per 1/4 cup

Tip: Always choose unsweetened versions.

Tips for Keto Success

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water. The keto diet can cause increased water loss.

Meal Prep

Prepare meals in advance to avoid impulsive non-keto choices.

Electrolytes

Maintain your sodium, potassium, and magnesium levels.

Track Macros

Track your macronutrients, especially in the beginning.
